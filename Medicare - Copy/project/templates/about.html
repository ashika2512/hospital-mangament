MEDICARE HEALTH CENTER

Our aim is “to bring healthcare of international standards within the reach of every individual. Our Clinic is committed to not only providing consistently superior quality health care services but also addressing the day-to-day health care needs of the family. To maximize convenience and comfort, our Clinic is an integrated model and offers facilities for Specialist Consultation, Diagnostics, Preventive Health Checks and Pharmacy, all under one roof.
 
The hospital features a 24-hour Emergency Room
5 operating theatres.
 Five types of intensive care units. (Medical,Surgical,Cardiac,Cardiothoracic,Neonatal.)
  Surgical facilities include cardiac catheterization labs   A surgical navigation system plus endoscopy Arthroscopy
Interventional radiology capabilities.

The hospital provides specialty medical services in Cardiology/ Cardiothoracic Surgery, Brain & Spine care, Orthopaedics & trauma, Women and Child Care, GI /Laparoscopy Surgery, Urology & Nephrology , Paediatric, Internal Medicine, Critical care Medicine, General surgery,  Dermatology, ENT, Ophthalmology, Dental & Maxillofacial Surgery, Reconstructive Surgery etc.
